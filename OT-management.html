OT Management

What is OT Management?

OT (Operation Theatre) Management is a system that helps hospitals manage everything needed in the operation theatre. It keeps track of medicines used during surgeries and checks if they are in stock. The system sends alerts for expired or low-stock medicines. It also handles billing, returns, and replacements of medicines. This makes the surgery process smooth, safe, and well-organized for both staff and patients.

Key Features of OT Management

✅

Real-Time Drug Inventory Check

Instantly verify medicine stock during prescription to avoid shortages or delays during surgeries.

💊

OP/IP Medicine Dispensing

Seamlessly dispense medicines to Outpatients/Inpatients with integrated billing for faster operations.

⏰

Expiry & Stock Alerts

Get alerts for soon-to-expire medicines and low stock levels, helping you stay ahead with proactive planning.

🔁

Return & Replacement

Track returned or replaced medicines efficiently with complete logs, reducing inventory mismatch issues.

Why Choose Our OT Management System?

Our OT Management System is built to make surgeries smoother, safer, and more efficient. Here’s how it helps your hospital:

  • Boost operational efficiency and safety inside the Operation Theatre
  • Ensure seamless medicine dispensing with accurate billing
  • Avoid stockouts and minimize expired drug usage
  • Improve accountability with full return/replacement logs
  • Integrate with pharmacy, billing, and patient records for a unified view
