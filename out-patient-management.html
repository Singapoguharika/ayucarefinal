Out-Patient Management

What is Out-Patient Management?

Our Out-Patient Management system helps hospitals handle everything from patient registration to doctor consultation and prescriptions in a smooth and simple way. It saves time, reduces waiting, and keeps all patient details safe and easy to find. Whether it's your first visit or a follow-up, the system makes the process faster and more organized. Doctors can view patient history easily and provide better care with all the needed info in one place. This system improves the overall experience for both patients and staff.

Key Features

  • Quick registration of new/existing patients
  • Appointment booking with preferred doctors
  • SMS/email notifications for appointments

What Happens in OPD?

Our Out-Patient Department (OPD) workflow ensures a smooth and comfortable experience for every patient. From registration at the reception to medical consultation and nurse-led vitals check, each step is well-managed to save time and improve quality of care.

  • Quick and friendly reception for registration
  • Comfortable and organized doctor consultations
  • Vitals checked and recorded by trained nurses
  • Minimal wait time with digital coordination
